Impact Passport gives NGOs, CSR teams, universities, and public-sector partners one verified system for volunteering, attendance, and credentials. It turns fragmented activity into a measurable, partner-ready network.

Why now

The ecosystem is fragmented and under-measured.

Volunteering still relies on manual coordination, unverified credentials, and scattered reporting across organizations.

📄

Unstructured Recognition

Impact lives in PDFs, spreadsheets, and screenshots. That makes it hard to trust, compare, or reuse across applications, CSR programs, and institutional reporting.

📉

Low Retention

Without a persistent identity and reward loop, volunteers have little reason to stay engaged beyond a single event or campaign.

📊

Manual CSR Ops

Companies and institutions still track participation manually, which creates audit friction and prevents real-time, verified impact reporting.

The product

One lifelong impact identity.

Every act of service is verified, attached to a permanent profile, and ready to be reused by partners who need trust.

For Volunteers

Discover opportunities via AI-matching. Build a verifiable resume of your social good to share with future employers and universities.

For NGOs

An end-to-end operating system. Create events, recruit vetted volunteers, track attendance via QR codes, and issue digital credentials instantly.

For Companies & Institutions

Launch volunteering programs, track participation in real time, and generate verified ESG or institutional impact reports.
